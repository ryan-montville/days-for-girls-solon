Days for Girls Guatemala Initiative - December 2025

Our Days for Girls team is building something lasting in Puerto Barrios, Guatemala — a partnership that goes beyond delivering menstrual kits. Together with local leaders, we're helping communities gain the knowledge, confidence, and tools to manage menstrual health with dignity.

In December 2025, we'll return to Guatemala to host educational sessions for both girls and boys, distribute 100 reusable menstrual kits, and continue training local men and women to lead these programs themselves. This next step is part of our long-term vision: a locally led education and distribution network that reaches villages year after year.

Your support makes this possible.

Every donation helps provide kits, materials, and education that change lives — keeping girls in school, breaking taboos, and strengthening entire communities.
